About Us & Services

Welcome to Haven Hub


We are a leading provider of care and support services, passionate about improving lives. With a dedicated team, we specialize in top-quality care and support for individuals in need, particularly in supported living housing. Our mission is to empower individuals to lead independent and fulfilling lives while prioritizing their well-being.

Tailored Accommodations


We understand that everyone has unique needs and preferences. That's why we offer a range of specially designed housing options to suit individual requirements. From cozy apartments to spacious shared houses, we ensure that each accommodation is comfortable, accessible, and promotes a sense of belonging.

Personalised Care Plans


We believe in person-centered care. Our team works closely with each individual to develop personalized care plans that address their specific goals, aspirations, and challenges. By focusing on their unique strengths and abilities, we foster an environment that encourages personal growth & development, hope and independence.

Holistic Approach


We take a holistic approach to care, recognizing that physical, emotional, and social well-being are interconnected. Our services encompass not only personal care but also access to healthcare professionals, recreational activities, and social integration opportunities. We strive to create a balanced and enriching lifestyle for all our clients.
